Top 10 Cyber Threats

Cyber security threats are malicious and deliberate attacks that seek to steal or damage data, or disrupt digital life in general.

They are an ever growing risk to individuals, businesses, and governments, cyber crime caused almost $1 trillion in damages in 2020 according to a report by McAfee.

Below are the top 10 cyber threats.

  1. Ransomware

    2. A type of malware attack which the attacker locks or encrypts the victim's data and threatens to publish or perpetually block access unless a ransom is paid.

  2. Supply Chain Attack

    3. An attack that seeks to damage an organisation by targetting less secure elements in it's supply chain.

  3. Malware

    4. Software intentionally designed to case disruption to a computer.

  4. Phishing

    5. A type of social engineering where an attacker sends a fraudulent message design to trick the recipient into revealing sensitive information.

  5. Spear Phishing

    6. A more sophisticated form of phishing where the attacker directly targets a specific organisation or individual.

  6. Man In The Middle Attack

    7. A cyber attack where the attacker secretly relays and alters communications between two parties.

  7. Denial Of Service

    8. This attack aims to flood systems, networks, and servers with massive amounts of traffic this rendering them unavailable to legitimate requests.

  8. SQL Injection

    9. A code injection technique used to attack data-driven applications.

  9. Zero-Day Exploits

    10. A software vulnerability for which the vendor of the flawed system is either unaware or has yet to make a patch.

  10. Cloud Security

    11. With an increasing reliance on the cloud for data storage comes a higher risk of a major breach.

Changing Work Patterns

With a recent shift to remote working, businesses now have to face a significant increase in their cyber attack surface as remote workers become targets themselves.
