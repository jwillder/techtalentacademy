Formula 1

I've loved Formula 1 my whole life and have watched many races through the years, indeed I haven't missed a single qualifying session or race since the start of the 2009 season!

My favourite ever race was the Canadian grand prix in 2011. That weekend I was in France to see the Le Mans 24 Hour race (that's not F1, but another amazing race). On the Sunday afternoon after the Le Mans race had finished the Canadian F1 race was broadcast live on a big screen. It was such an amazing atmosphere to be around so many like-minded motor sport fans plus the race itself was incredible due to Jenson Button coming from last position to win the race.

Favourite Driver: Fernando Alonso

Favourite Team: McLaren
